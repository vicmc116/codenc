Hard Disk Sentinal

HDSentinel) is a hard disk monitoring software with highest possible support of HDD/SSD/SSHD (hybrid) drives. Its goal is to find, test, diagnose and repair hard disk drive problems, report and display SSD and HDD health, performance degradations and failures.

Malwarebytes

CCleaner

The world's most popular PC cleaner it protects your privacy and makes your computer faster and more secure! CCleaner's new Health Check analyzes your PC and recommends quick fixes, then automatically tunes and updates it so it starts and runs faster, and more secure.

Chrome from Google
